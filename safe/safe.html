# DefiYield Safe

DefiYield Safe is a Machine Learning tool that automatically detects and alerts users in case they have approved or are going to approve token allowance for malicious smart contracts. It was designed as a part of the DefiYield Ecosystem. Such a tool is important as it prevents people from investing in scam projects. It is especially crucial for newcomers in the DeFi sector which might not have enough knowledge yet.
