# Introduction

# DefiYield

DefiYield is a decentralized protocol for cross-chain asset management. Our mission is to facilitate search for the best profit opportunities in the yield farming space and make the investment process safer for all users through better control over yield farming costs and featuring possible risks of participation in different DeFi projects. We achieve these goals through offering a wide range of effective and user-friendly tools that are regularly updated and improved with an eye to market trends and user requests.

# Vision

DeFi is one of the most talked about and hyped topics in the cryptocurrency space now. It attracts millions of investors as the industry brings a lot of impressive profit opportunities with new Defi projects emerging every day.

Taking into account that there is a lack of clear and structured information about arising projects and rug pulls happen with high frequency, DefiYield started its activity as a fighter against scams. The project strove for uncovering possible risks associated with terms encoded in their smart contracts to all yield farmers.

Since then, DefiYield has widened its scope emphasizing development of the Cross-Chain Asset Management Dashboard. So now, the goal of DefiYield is to be an entry point for DeFi.
