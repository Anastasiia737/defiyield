# Fiat on Ramp

DefiYield utilizes the RAMP protocol to offer users swapping fiat to crypto in the fastest and easiest way. This is achieved through aggregation of various liquidity sources and payment methods in one place. As crypto is getting more attention and the growing potential of its adoption is proven, more and more people will need to get into crypto fast and easy. DefiYield is ready to serve as a decentralized Coinbase. With self-explanatory UI and clear transaction terms, it’s a perfect solution for any crypto newbie. Other advantages of the solution include approachable fees and wide selection of supported payment methods available within the app. Available features

Purchasing Crypto with Open Banking Direct connections to bank APIs allows verifying transactions and executing payments. Data from closed banks ledgers are oraclised and used as a payment settlement basis, enabling users to exchange fiat for crypto fast, with higher limits and affordable fees.

Purchasing Crypto with Debit Cards and ApplePay The purchase process is supposed to take less than 5 minutes. Only a basic KYC-verification is required to start the service. The method is available for all EEA countries.

SEPA Transfers The option is available to users having a EUR denominated bank account. On average, processing of these transactions takes 24 - 48 hours.

Selling Crypto with Open Banking Users can sell their crypto to get profits while providing liquidity to the network. After a connection to API of a seller’s bank account is settled, buyers pay for crypto directly to the seller’s account. Transaction security is ensured as crypto of sellers is locked with a smart contract and released automatically to buyers only after corresponding bank transfers are completed.
