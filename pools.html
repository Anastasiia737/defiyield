# Pools and Vaults

Users can navigate to the “Pools” and "Vaults" sections to manage funds staked in liquidity pools and yield farming vaults.

Available options:

  • Rebalancing
  • Adding liquidity
  • Withdrawing liquidity
  • Claiming Rewards
  • Reviewing staked amount, impermanent loss and earned fees
