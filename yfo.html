# Yield Farming Optimizer

Yield Farming Optimizer by DefiYield is a solution for automated portfolio management based on a list of funds placement strategies that can be selected by investors depending on their preferences. The tool can be seen as a set of smart contracts bound to appropriate strategies that interact with deposited funds to generate yield. Every YF strategy has a library of rules that are applicable only to defined asset types. Using YFO has a wide range of financial and user experience benefits compared to manual funds placement across separate yield farming protocols. They are:

  • Accessing profit opportunities associated with growth of the whole DeFi industry in one place without a need for manual funds rotation. Considering the crypto market is active 24/7, automation of yield strategies is required to pull maximum out of the constantly changing market environment
  • Diversification of yield farming strategies for minimization of smart contract risks and effects of market fluctuations.
  • Ratios of portfolio assets are adjusted automatically based on the risk-yield relation.
  • Optimized gas costs due to reduction of rebalance fees

Risk-Reward Optimizer The strategy enables automated funds rotation across pools based on the risk adjustment of the expected yield. The risk level is estimated with consideration of financial risks, associated with collateral and liquidity, smart contract risks and level of centralization of used protocols. In order to include all these risk factors into the automated asset management model, the protocol reads on-chain and off-chain data. When the risk-reward ratio of a portfolio changes compared to the initial, selected level, underlying assets are automatically rebalanced and relocated by smart contracts of the appropriate strategy to ensure stability of earnings and control over the biggest DeFi risks.

Yield maximization With the Yield Maximization strategy, deposited funds are automatically relocated into pools with the highest current yield. Considering the yield farming market is exploding, it's hard for investors to keep up with new profit opportunities: manual rotation is very time-consuming and the possibility of human error is high.

Stablecoins strategy DefiYield created access to leveraged Curve 3Pool, consisting of DAI, USDC and USDT. Users can enjoy absence of impermanent loss along with optimized gas costs.
