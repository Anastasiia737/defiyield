# Cross-Chain spot trading

The Advanced Trading tool by DefiYield was designed specifically to meet a wide range of needs experienced by traders while performing intraday, swing and long-term strategies. All features are built in a fully decentralized manner: only users have access to traded funds, can place or cancel orders, and all transactions are executed by smart contracts without involvement of any intermediary.
Key Features

  • Real-time trading charts
  • Limit orders
  • Smart Contacts enabling asset leveraging: traders can increase their purchasing power more than tenfold for both long and short positions. The service is provided with transparent fees and stable liquidity.
  • Easy setup: users only need to connect Metamask or other preferred crypto wallet to be ready to start trading;
  • Fast withdrawals
  • Unlimited potential for short positions. Users can short any listed asset in any quantity, conditioned exclusively by available purchasing power. With DefiYield, there is no need to wait for bullish market developments. Users can make profits at all market phases: during uptrend, sidewave movements or even in downtrend.
  • Minimized slippage by placing market orders compared to popular AMMs. When AMMs use constant product (x*y=k), users face high slippages. This keeps them away from DEXes and they stay at traditional centralized exchanges. DefiYield adjusts the k-parameter algorithmically, ensuring minimized slippage for traders.
